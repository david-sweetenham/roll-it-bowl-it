About

Roll It & Bowl It — Version 1.0

Built with Python, Flask, SQLite, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks harmed.

Dice cricket is a tabletop tradition — each delivery is decided by rolling physical dice, combining chance with cricket logic. Roll It & Bowl It brings that experience to the screen: every ball triggers a multi-stage virtual roll that determines whether it's a dot, a boundary, or a wicket.

Beyond single matches, the game gives you a full cricket universe: create teams and players, run bilateral series and World Cups, build a living world calendar, and watch an Almanack of records accumulate over time. The dice decide the moment; you decide the era.
